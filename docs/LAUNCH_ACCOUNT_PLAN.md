# BuilderPerks Launch Account Plan

## Goal

Create the launch surfaces before the big launch, but do not launch broadly until BuilderPerks has enough proof to survive developer scrutiny.

## Launch Surfaces

### Product Hunt

Use Product Hunt after the contextual matcher is live and at least 10 real builder feedback notes exist.

Prep:

- Create or confirm a real maker account.
- Add founder/maker profile details.
- Prepare product URL: https://builderperks.netlify.app/
- Prepare GitHub URL: https://github.com/barneywohl/builderperks
- Prepare Chrome beta/release link.
- Prepare a launch gallery: screenshot, short GIF/video, trust/control screenshot, dashboard screenshot.
- Prepare first maker comment explaining the experiment.
- Schedule launch instead of posting cold.

Listing draft:

- Name: BuilderPerks
- Tagline: Contextual devtool perks during AI wait time
- Topics: Developer Tools, Chrome Extensions, AI Agents, Productivity, Marketing
- Short description: BuilderPerks detects what you are building during AI wait states and surfaces one relevant devtool perk, credit, or resource.

Maker comment:

```text
Hey Product Hunt, I built BuilderPerks to test a simple question:

AI builders spend a lot of time waiting on Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, and agent tools. Can that wait time become useful without becoming annoying?

The current beta is intentionally conservative:
- one card at a time
- local/contextual matching
- no advertiser scripts
- dismiss, pause, disable, and daily cap controls
- manual approval for offers

I am looking for blunt feedback: useful, neutral, or annoying?
```

### Hacker News

Use only after the contextual matcher is live and there is a technical story.

Potential title:

`Show HN: BuilderPerks - contextual devtool perks during AI wait time`

HN angle:

- Local context matching.
- No cross-site profile.
- No advertiser scripts.
- One card only.
- Asking whether this is useful or annoying.

Use X for build-in-public updates and highly relevant replies.

Do not mass reply. Reply only when the thread is already about AI coding workflow, wait time, tool credits, or devtool discovery.

## Account Rules

- Use real maker/founder identity where possible.
- Avoid fake traction.
- Do not ask people to create accounts just to vote.
- Ask for feedback, not empty upvotes.
- Do not launch until we can answer comments all day.

## Readiness Gate

Do not launch on Product Hunt/HN until:

- Contextual matcher shipped. Done in extension `0.2.0`.
- Chrome beta ZIP verified.
- Trust/privacy page live.
- 10+ written builder feedback notes.
- 3+ advertiser conversations or at least 1 explicit pilot interest.
- Demo video or GIF ready.
- First 20 supporters/users know to give feedback on launch day.
