## Positioning Decision

Do not lead with "ads in AI tools."

Lead with:

> BuilderPerks turns dead AI wait time into one locally matched devtool perk. Builders stay in control. Advertisers get proof of real build intent, not vanity impressions.

This is sharper than generic developer ads because BuilderPerks is about build-moment intent:

- Carbon/BuySellAds: trusted curated developer reach.
- EthicalAds: privacy-first contextual developer demand.
- daily.dev: daily developer feed attention.
- BuilderPerks: active build workflow intent inside AI coding surfaces.
